Overview

During my study abroad program in Barcelona, my International Marketing class was tasked with conceptualizing a potential NFL expansion into Europe. Our team selected Dublin, Ireland, developing a full market entry strategy that addressed city selection, logistics, cultural considerations, and target audiences, framed as a pitch from a small creative agency to the NFL board. I led the development of the brand identity, collaborating closely with teammates to ensure it aligned with our proposed operational plan, audience personas, and marketing initiatives. The project emphasized strategic thinking, cross-cultural insight, and brand-building within a real-world business context.
